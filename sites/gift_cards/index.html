Buying an OpenTable Gift Card: What's the Occasion?

A data science analysis for the gifts team

We looked at nearly 800,000 snippets of text that people put in their gift cards to get a sense of why they are buying them. Since most of these reasons are phrases, such as "Happy 36th Birthday", we used a data-driven phrase detection algorithm inspired by this paper .
Further analyis can be performed by clustering these phrases according to concepts (such as birthdays, special days, other life events etc.), but the analysis below seems sufficient to get a very good sense of why gifts cards are being gifted!
First, we show the top phrases as a word cloud, which is followed by the table which shows a phrase and the fraction of times it appears in the corpus as a percentage. Enjoy!
