LA Restaurant Week Reviews

This table shows the best three reviews from the top 10 highly reviewed restaurants during restaurant week (RW). This is how it was done:
  1. For every RID participating in RW, we find all reviews that mention RW.
  2. We score each review using our sentiment model (the score varies between -1.0 i.e. extremely negative and +1.0 i.e. extremely positive)
  3. For each RID we compute a shrinkage adjusted average sentiment score: (sum of scores)/(Number of reviews + 5), where we penalize RIDs that have less than 5 reviews. We rank the restaurants by this avg score and pick the top 10 RIDs.
  4. For each of the top 10 RIDs, we surface the top 3 highest sentiment reviews.
